About us

Welcome! We are Zitem and Ina, students of the Water Management program at HZ University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands, deeply passionate about regeneration and sustainable practices.

Within our E-book, you'll discover a collection of personally experienced regenerative experiences, ranging from accommodations to bird walks and unique cafés and restaurants. These places have all been visited by us, ensuring an authentic and reliable guide for your journey.

If you share our passion for regeneration and sustainable living, we invite you to explore the pages of our E-book. It's a guide crafted with care, aimed at providing you with meaningful regenerative experiences that not only enrich your travels but also contribute positively to the destinations you visit.

Check out our E-book and discover the beauty of regenerative tourism firsthand!
